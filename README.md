# 🚦 Smart Route

### AI-Powered Smart Traffic Management System

Smart Route is an intelligent traffic management platform designed to improve urban mobility through real-time traffic monitoring, smart signal control, route optimization, and AI-driven traffic analytics. The system helps reduce congestion, optimize travel time, and support emergency vehicle prioritization, contributing to smarter and more efficient cities.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 🚗 Real-Time Traffic Monitoring

Monitor live traffic conditions and visualize traffic density across different routes and intersections.

### 🧠 AI-Powered Traffic Analytics

Analyze historical and real-time traffic data to identify congestion patterns and predict traffic flow.

### 🚦 Intelligent Signal Management

Dynamically optimize traffic signal timings based on traffic density and road conditions.

### 🗺️ Smart Route Optimization

Generate efficient travel routes by considering current traffic conditions and congestion levels.

### 🚑 Emergency Vehicle Prioritization

Provide faster clearance and optimized routes for ambulances and emergency response vehicles.

### 📊 Interactive Dashboard

Visualize traffic insights using charts, graphs, maps, and real-time analytics.

### 📈 Data-Driven Decision Making

Enable authorities and administrators to make informed decisions through actionable traffic intelligence.
